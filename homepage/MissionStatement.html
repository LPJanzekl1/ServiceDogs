All About Service Dogs

Mission Statement

After starting my own journey into finding a Service Dog, I grew frustrated looking for information on the process. I didn't know what to expect or how to reach out to others with similar experiences. Because of this eye opening journey of getting a Service Dog, becoming a Handler, and the research involved, I decided to make this website to hopefully help others get information easier and without feeling stuck at deadends of websites.
